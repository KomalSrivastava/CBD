About Us

CBD Corporate Services

CBD is an award-winning, end-to-end corporate services firm with offices in Riyadh, Dubai, Doha, and Muscat. Renowned for offering a comprehensive suite of market entry services and guidance to entities at the forefront of international trade and commerce, CBD excels in facilitating foreign companies and investors to navigate the GCC market. Our expertise encompasses in-depth feasibility studies, company formation, business structuring, secure corporate nominee partners or agents, licensing, PRO/visa assistance, accounting & advisory, relocation, Employer of Record (EoR), and compliance.

Key Services Offered

Why Choose Us

Company Setup Services: We provide end-to-end support for establishing your business in Saudi Arabia, including Company Registration, Licensing, MISA Licence Support, setting up your Regional Headquarters (RHQ), and all kinds of visa and iqama services.

Post-Incorporation Services: Our expert team ensures your business remains compliant and operationally sound in Saudi Arabia with essential post-incorporation support, including assistance with MHRSD, GOSI, GAZT, and VAT certification, management of additional licences like media and civil defence. Additionally, we assist with portal registrations for Muqeem, QIWA, and Mudad.
